    - real
    - 0.0

    - `MMin`

    - 0.1
    -

*   - 12
    - `MMax`
    - real
    - 0.9
    - 
    - Maximum value of modulation index.
*   - 13
    - `IACMax`

    - 2.0

    - Maximum value of AC line current, per-unit of nominal.
*   - 14
    - `IDCMax`
    - real
    - 2.0
    -

*   - 15
    - `VACRef`

    - 0.0
    -

*   - 16
    - `PACRef`
    - real
    - 0.0

*   - 17
    - `QACRef`
    - real
    - 0.0
    -

*   - 18
    - `VDCRef`

    -
